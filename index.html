Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Streamza?

No. Streamza works without any sign-up. You connect to streaming platforms using a stream key from that platform — your data stays on your device.

Which platforms can I stream to?

YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, Kick, Restream, and any service that accepts a Custom RTMP/RTMPS stream key.

Where do I find my stream key?

In your platform's live dashboard — for example, YouTube Studio → Go Live → Stream, or your Twitch Creator Dashboard → Settings → Stream. See our Support page for per-platform steps.

Is Streamza free?

Yes — core streaming and screen recording are free. A PRO subscription (coming soon) unlocks higher quality options such as 60 fps, 1440p screen recording, higher bitrate and studio audio.

Does Streamza collect my data?

We do not collect your personal data or your stream content on our servers. Your broadcast goes directly to the platform you choose. Read the full Privacy Policy.
