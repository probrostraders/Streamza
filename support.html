Getting started

  1. On the home screen, choose your platform (YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, Kick, Restream, or Custom RTMP).
  2. Paste your stream key — the server URL is pre-filled for popular platforms.
  3. Choose your source: Camera, Screen, or a Video file.
  4. Tap Go Live. To record locally instead, use Screen Record from the bottom bar.

Troubleshooting

“Stream key rejected” or it won't connect

Copy a fresh key from your platform, make sure your live event is actually started on the platform, and confirm the server URL is correct. Some networks block standard RTMP — try a different network if needed.

The picture looks soft or keeps buffering

Your upload speed may be too low. Use Tools → Upload Speed Test to measure it and apply the recommended settings, or lower the resolution/bitrate in Settings. Keep Adaptive Bitrate on.

A video file won't stream

The file must be H.264 video + AAC audio (most MP4s are). Try another file or convert it first.

How do I record my screen instead of streaming?

Tap Screen Record on the bottom navigation. You can add a facecam, draw on screen, and use Game Mode for smooth gameplay capture. Recordings save to your device gallery.

Subscription & billing questions

Manage or cancel anytime in Google Play → Subscriptions. To restore a purchase on a new device, use the “Restore Purchases” option in the app. See subscription terms.

How do I delete my data?

Streamza has no account; all data is on your device. Clear it via Android Settings → Apps → Streamza → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app. See the Data Deletion page.
